The Khwahishein School : An evening school for the under-privileged

An initiative by us which is complimentary to the RTE act. Sponsored by the Narain Sri Foundation, we see The Khwahishein School as an opportunity to make underpriviliged children school-ready so as to reduce drop out rates and give them a fair chance to become educated and successful citizens. With an emphasis on overall education, we are striving to imbibe good morals and practices in our students in these crucial years of physical and mental development.

Araatrika and Samman

These projects were started by us in the school run by Rashtriya Hit Sewa Sangathan (RHSS). In these projects, we collaborate with schools that are being run for underprivileged children to take overall development sessions. These sessions include inculcating in them the importance of dreams, and how can they achieve their goals, safety and also to promote health and hygiene amongst the kids. Araatrika is the project for underprivileged adolescent females, with the focus being laid upon their health. Sammaan is the project wherein the focus stays upon inculcating equality for both the genders, to teach kids how to respect the other gender.

Khairaat

It is our annual donation drive, wherein we collect essential commodities from donors and identify sections of people who need them. We then proceed to the distribution of collected commodities to the people in dire need of them. We have recently helped resettle Kashmiris who were displaced due to the rampant flood in J&K though this project by donating food and clothes in ample amounts.

Monthly Theme Projects

Every month, we decide upon a theme to identify a social issue and work on it. In the month of March, we decided to focus on enabling people suffering from physical and mental disabilities. We made multiple visits to institutions like Deepashram , which cater to such individuals and tried to address the issues faced by them. In addition to that, we organised some fun activities for them to enjoy. This monthly theme format allows us to cater to a wider range of society in an efficient manner and allows us to identify new areas where we can be of assistance.
