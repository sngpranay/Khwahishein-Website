verb

A word that describes the feeling of wanting something.

ABOUT US

We all wish for a lot of things in our life. The feeling of joy experienced when these wishes are fulfilled is immense. What supersedes that feeling of happiness, is the feeling of satisfaction for being an enabler for such happiness. That's who we are and what we do. We fulfil the wishes of those who only have humanity to depend on, and we never let them down! Come join us in ensuring that all unheard wishes are fulfilled!

PROJECTS

The world is an extended society inhabited by social beings like us- God's chosen creation. Humanity, on the other hand is a beautiful string of pearl's if it snaps, the entire universe falls apart- scattered at the hands of the Lord's beloved. KHWAHISHEIN is an endeavour to realise the value of the bond amongst mankind and thus treasure the string of pearls entrusted upon us by the almighty. The team aspires to help all those who deserve to nurture a happier heart, and wear a healthier smile. It aims to raise awareness amidst society that it is our conscience alone which can bring about a change in the lives of the under privileged and structure a more satisfied environment.. because peace walks hand in hand with satiation. It is a big big world.. and individual efforts are but tiny steps to the destination. If many hands interlock their fingers into a tight grip, the mission is easier to achieve. This will help to transfer energies, share ideas, e xcavate contacts and work towards a greater cause.
